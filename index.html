Controls:

W: move the box up
A: move the box left
S: move the box down
D: move the box right
R: reset the box

How to play:

The Rules are simple.
It's similar to snake,
but instead of growing
in length, you grow in
size. The red bites
decrease your speed,
the green bites
increase it. The blue
bites increase your
size, the yellow
decrease it.
