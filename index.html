Controls:

W: move the box up
A: move the box left
S: move the box down
D: move the box right
R: reset the box

How to play:

This game is
similar to snake;
however, instead of
growing in length, you
grow in size.
Red bites
decrease your speed.
Green bites
increase your speed.
Blue bites
increase your size.
Yellow bites
decrease your size.
